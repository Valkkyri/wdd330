Master Mobile UX

Design for the smallest and least powerful device that will be using your app.

"The simple guideline is whatever you are doing - do mobile first." - Eric Schmidt (World Mobile Congress Keynote, 2012)

Do mobile first: this is great for committed web developers, but I see some issues with this. Mainly, that there are a LOT of people who use WYSIWYG editors for their sites. I build sites that small business owners can manage, they are going to make all their changes on a desktop or laptop. I can give them the tools to make their edits in the 'responsive editing' option, but most WYSIWYG editors are not built mobile first. When we build, we build on a computer, it's the easiest medium, but I'm not sure the average small business owner is going to take the time to use the mobile first approach.
